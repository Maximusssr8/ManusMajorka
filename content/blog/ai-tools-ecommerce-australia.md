## How to Evaluate Any AI Tool for Your Business

Before subscribing to any AI tool, run it through this evaluation framework:

### 1. Does It Actually Save Time or Money?

Calculate the ROI. If a $50/month tool saves you 10 hours of work per month, and your time is worth $30/hour, that's a $250 return on a $50 investment. If it only saves 30 minutes, it's probably not worth it.

### 2. Does It Work for the Australian Market?

Check whether the tool:
- Supports AUD pricing
- Uses Australian English (or can be configured to)
- Has Australian market data
- Understands GST and local tax requirements
- Provides relevant local benchmarks

### 3. Does It Integrate With Your Existing Stack?

A tool that doesn't connect to your Shopify store, your ad platforms, or your email system creates data silos that reduce its usefulness.

### 4. What's the Learning Curve?

The most powerful tool is useless if you never learn to use it properly. Consider whether the tool is intuitive enough for your technical level.

### 5. Is There a Free Tier or Trial?

Always test before committing. Most AI tools offer free tiers or trials — use them to evaluate the tool with your actual products and workflow before paying.

**Australian regulatory environment**: The Australian government is actively developing AI regulation frameworks. Sellers using AI tools should stay informed about requirements around transparency, data privacy, and consumer protection.
