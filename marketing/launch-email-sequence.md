**Subject line:** How I found a $500/day product in one afternoon

A few months back, I sat down with Majorka open on my laptop — coffee from my usual spot in Mermaid Beach — and decided to run the full pipeline on a niche I'd been curious about: home organisation products.

Here's exactly what happened:

**Step 1: Research (22 minutes)**
I used the Product Research tool and told it my constraints — budget under $5K, margins above 40%, lightweight for shipping. Majorka's AI scanned the market and surfaced 14 product opportunities. One stood out immediately: a modular drawer organiser with strong search volume but only 3 decent competitors on Amazon AU.

**Step 2: Validate (15 minutes)**
Ran it through the Demand Validator. Search trend was up 35% year-over-year. Average review count on competing products was low (under 200). The profit calculator showed $18.40 net margin per unit at a $44.95 price point. Green lights across the board.

**Step 3: Build (45 minutes)**
Used the Brand DNA tool to generate a brand identity. The Listing Generator wrote 5 variations of the product listing. The Website Generator built a basic landing page I could use for testing. All of this — brand, copy, website — in under an hour.

**Step 4: Launch (1 week)**
Majorka's launch tools gave me a go-to-market plan: initial PPC budget, keyword targets, email sequence for early buyers, and a social content calendar.

**Step 5: Optimize (ongoing)**
Two weeks post-launch, I used the analytics tools to see what was converting and what wasn't. Adjusted the main image, tweaked the title based on search term data, and cut two underperforming ad campaigns.

**The result:** Within 6 weeks, that product was doing $500/day consistently. One product. Found in an afternoon.
