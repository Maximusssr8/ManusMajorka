## Campaign Notes

- **Objective:** Conversions (sign-ups) via Advantage+ Shopping or Advantage+ App campaigns
- **Audience:** Broad targeting with Advantage+ (let Meta's algorithm optimize). Retargeting via website visitor custom audiences (7-day and 30-day windows).
- **Budget allocation:** 70% prospecting (Angles 1–3), 30% retargeting (RT1–RT3)
- **Testing plan:** Run all 9 prospecting ads in one Advantage+ campaign. Let Meta auto-allocate budget to top performers for 5–7 days before pausing underperformers.
- **Creative refresh:** Swap lowest performers every 2 weeks. Rotate new hooks and angles monthly.
- **Tracking:** UTM parameters on all ad links — `utm_source=meta&utm_medium=paid&utm_campaign={angle}&utm_content={format}`
