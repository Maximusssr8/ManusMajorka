## Best Launch Day & Time Guidance

### Day
**Tuesday is the optimal launch day for Product Hunt.**
- Highest traffic day of the week (Tuesday/Wednesday)
- Avoids Monday slowness and Friday drops
- Product Hunt community most active midweek
- Gives you Tuesday + Wednesday to accumulate votes before weekend decline

**Second choice:** Wednesday

**Avoid:** Friday, Saturday, Sunday (significantly lower traffic)

### Time
**Launch at exactly 12:01 AM PT (Pacific Time) / 8:01 AM UTC**
- Product Hunt's day resets at midnight PT
- Launching right at midnight gives you the full 24-hour cycle
- This means your launch goes live when US West Coast is asleep, Europe/Asia is awake — you can build early momentum from global audience before US wakes up
- By the time US is online (7-9 AM PT), you already have votes = more credible in trending

### Day-Before Preparation
- Pre-write all messages (don't improvise)
- Warm up the email list with a "big announcement tomorrow" teaser
- Line up 5-10 people who commit to being awake at midnight to upvote first
- Prepare all gallery images, GIF demo, and description in draft 48h in advance
- Reach out to Hunter (someone with Product Hunt followers to hunt your product)

### What NOT To Do
- Don't ask for upvotes in a way that violates PH guidelines (no "vote for me" in public posts)
- Don't use upvote exchange groups (PH detects and removes them)
- Don't launch and disappear — stay engaged all day
- Don't change your listing after it goes live (algorithm hit)
- Don't forget to apply for the daily newsletter by engaging early
