## 2. Top 5 Domain Ranking

**Scoring criteria (weighted):**
- Brandability (30%): How clean, memorable, and professional the domain sounds
- Extension Trust (25%): User trust and perceived legitimacy of the TLD
- SEO Potential (20%): Search engine preference and click-through impact
- Length Score (25%): Shorter = better for recall, typing, word-of-mouth

| 2 | **majorka.io** | 8 | 8 | 7 | 9 | **8.00** |
| 3 | **majorka.app** | 7 | 7 | 7 | 9 | **7.50** |
| 4 | **getmajorka.com** | 7 | 9 | 8 | 6 | **7.45** |
| 5 | **usemajorka.com** | 6 | 9 | 8 | 5 | **6.85** |

### Scoring Rationale

**majorka.ai (8.55)** — The .ai extension directly communicates the product's core value proposition. It has become the gold standard for AI companies (e.g., perplexity.ai, jasper.ai). Short, clean, and instantly tells visitors what the product is about.

**majorka.io (8.00)** — Established and trusted in the SaaS/tech space. Widely adopted by developer tools and platforms. Slightly less specific than .ai for an AI product but broadly respected.

**majorka.app (7.50)** — Modern and clean. Enforces HTTPS. Slightly less established than .io in the B2B SaaS space but growing in adoption.

**getmajorka.com (7.45)** — Gets the .com trust advantage but the prefix adds length and reduces brandability. Solid fallback if a short .com is important for marketing materials.

**usemajorka.com (6.85)** — Similar to getmajorka.com but "use" is a weaker call-to-action than "get". Functional but not inspiring.

## 3. Recommendation

**Primary domain: majorka.ai**

Reasoning:
- **Brand alignment:** Majorka is an AI-powered product. The .ai extension immediately communicates this without needing to explain it.
- **Industry standard:** .ai has become the de facto extension for AI companies. Users in the target market (ecommerce operators looking for AI tools) will recognize and trust it.
- **Brevity:** At 10 characters total, it is short, easy to type, and easy to share verbally.
- **Marketing clarity:** "majorka.ai" in ad copy, social bios, and pitch decks instantly signals the product category.
- **Growing SEO parity:** Google has confirmed that new TLDs like .ai receive no ranking penalty compared to .com.

**Secondary domain: majorka.io**

Use as a redirect or for developer-facing content if needed. The .io extension provides a strong backup with broad tech credibility.

## 4. Australian Domain Note

**majorka.com.au** — Requires an active Australian Business Number (ABN) to register. If Max operates Majorka through an Australian business entity (Gold Coast based), this domain should also be secured for local market trust. Registration is restricted to ABN/ACN holders and is managed through accredited .au registrars (e.g., VentraIP, Crazy Domains, NetRegistry).
