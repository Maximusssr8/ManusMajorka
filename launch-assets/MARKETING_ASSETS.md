Products that double as gifts (for partners, parents, colleagues) have 40% higher Q4 performance and smoother year-round sales. Think: personalised items, premium presentation, under $50.

If the product doesn't look good in a 3-second video clip, skip it. The products winning in 2026 are inherently visual — transformations, before/after, satisfying unboxing.
