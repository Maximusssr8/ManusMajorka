## 3. Full Description

Majorka is an AI-powered ecommerce operating system purpose-built for Australian dropshippers and online sellers. It brings together 20+ specialised AI tools across every stage of your business -- from finding your first winning product to scaling profitably -- all in one place.

### The Problem

Starting and running a dropshipping business in Australia is harder than it needs to be. You're juggling a dozen different tools, paying for subscriptions you barely use, and spending hours on research that should take minutes. Most existing platforms are built for the US market, leaving Aussie sellers to figure out AUD pricing, local supplier logistics, and AU consumer behaviour on their own.

### What Majorka Does

Majorka organises your entire ecommerce workflow into five clear stages:

- **Research** -- AI-powered product research, niche analysis, and trend spotting tailored to the Australian market
- **Validate** -- Profit calculators with AUD margins, saturation checkers, and competitor analysis tools
- **Build** -- Store copy generators, product description writers, and brand identity tools
- **Launch** -- Launch checklists, ad copy generators, and marketing strategy planners
- **Optimise** -- Performance analysis, pricing optimisation, and scaling recommendations

Every tool is powered by Claude AI, with contextual memory that learns about your business as you use it. Ask it anything -- it remembers your niche, your budget, your goals, and gives you advice that actually makes sense for your situation.

### What Makes Majorka Different

**Built for Australia, not bolted on.** This is not a US tool with a currency converter slapped on top. Majorka understands GST, AUD margins, Australian consumer trends, local shipping considerations, and the specific challenges Aussie dropshippers face.

**All-in-one, not another subscription.** Instead of paying for separate tools for product research, copywriting, competitor analysis, and profit calculations, Majorka brings everything under one roof. One login, one interface, one AI that knows your business.

**AI that remembers you.** Majorka's chat system maintains conversation memory across sessions. The more you use it, the better it understands your business and the more relevant its suggestions become.

### Pricing

- **Free** -- Get started with core tools, no credit card required
- **Pro ($49 AUD/mo)** -- Full tool access and priority AI
- **Business ($149 AUD/mo)** -- Everything, plus advanced features and priority support

Majorka is live now at [manus-majorka.vercel.app](https://manus-majorka.vercel.app). Built solo by Max Scutella on the Gold Coast.

## 6. Maker's Comment Replies

### Q: "What's the pricing? Is there a free tier?"

> Great question! There's a free tier so you can try the core tools without a credit card. If you want the full suite, Pro is $49 AUD/month and Business is $149 AUD/month. I wanted to keep it accessible -- especially since a lot of Aussie dropshippers are just starting out and don't want to commit to expensive subscriptions before they've validated their idea. Happy to answer any specifics about what's included in each tier!

### Q: "Is this only for Australian sellers?"

> It's built with Australia in mind first -- AUD pricing, GST calculations, AU market insights -- but honestly, a lot of the tools (product research, copywriting, competitor analysis) work regardless of where you're based. The AI adapts to your context. That said, the Australia-specific features are where Majorka really shines compared to alternatives. Expanding with deeper support for other markets is definitely on the roadmap, so if you're outside AU, I'd love to hear what localisation would matter most to you.

### Q: "What AI model powers this?"

> Majorka runs on Anthropic's Claude (Sonnet). I chose Claude over GPT specifically because of its stronger reasoning for business analysis and its ability to maintain context across longer conversations. The system also uses conversation memory, so it builds up an understanding of your specific business over time -- your niche, products, budget, goals. It's not just a chatbot wrapper; each of the 20+ tools has a specialised system prompt tuned for that specific task.

### Q: "How do you handle data privacy?"

> Good question and something I take seriously. User data is stored securely via Supabase (which runs on AWS infrastructure). Your business information is only used to personalise your own AI experience -- it's never shared with other users or used for training. Auth is handled through Supabase Auth with JWT tokens, and database access uses row-level security policies so users can only ever access their own data. I'm a solo founder and I'd rather have a small, trustworthy product than cut corners on privacy.
