30-day money-back guarantee. Annual discounts available.

## Key Takeaways for Majorka Positioning

### 1. The AU Gap is WIDE OPEN
Not a single competitor offers any Australian-specific features. No AU supplier databases, no AUD pricing, no GST guidance, no AU market data, no AU shipping estimates. This is Majorka's strongest differentiator.

### 2. Nobody Covers the Full Journey
- Mixo and Durable stop at "build a website"
- DimeADozen stops at "validate the idea"
- NicheScraper and Sell The Trend start at "find products" and skip everything before
- **Only Majorka covers Research → Validate → Build → Launch → Optimise**

### 3. AI Quality is a Differentiator
- Most competitors use AI for simple generation (website content, product descriptions)
- None offer a persistent AI assistant that learns your business context
- None use conversation memory or personalised prompts
- Majorka's Claude-powered AI chat with memory is genuinely unique in this space

### 4. Sell The Trend is the Strongest Direct Competitor
- Most feature overlap with Majorka's vision
- Well-funded, established, active development
- But: no validation phase, no strategic AI, no AU focus, expensive at scale ($465 AUD/mo for Pro+)
- Majorka should study their NEXUS product research and 1-click fulfillment as benchmarks

### 5. Pricing Opportunity
- The market ranges from free to $465 AUD/mo
- DimeADozen proves people pay $91 AUD for a single validation report
- A Majorka subscription at $30-80 AUD/mo covering 20+ tools across all stages would be extremely competitive
- Position against the "you'd need 3-4 of these tools" argument (NicheScraper + DimeADozen + Durable = $170-320 AUD/mo)

### 6. Threats to Watch
- Sell The Trend adding AI chat or validation features
- Durable expanding into ecommerce (currently service-business focused)
- New AU-focused entrants copying the localisation angle
- Shopify's own AI tools improving to cover research/validation
